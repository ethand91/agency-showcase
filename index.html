Features

Everything a VTuber
Could Ever Need

15 modules in one sidebar. A complete production studio, scheduling engine, and analytics platform — self-hosted and yours.

🎬

Studio — Live2D Recording

Headless browser-based Live2D recording — no GPU needed. Trigger expressions with emotion markers (*happy*, *surprised*), add TTS voice, background music, and auto-burn subtitles into the final MP4.

PlaywrightFFmpegWebM → MP4TTS Preview
🎤

Singing — Full Cover Pipeline

Upload a song → separate vocals with Demucs → convert voice via RVC API → mix with FFmpeg → generate lip-sync data → render the final video. Karaoke creation and LRC/MusicXML notation supported.

DemucsRVC APILip-syncyt-dlp
📡

Live — Dual VTuber Streaming

Run two characters on one stream with AI auto-banter, real-time Twitch & Kick chat listeners, speech queuing, and an OBS browser source. Direct FFmpeg RTMP streaming also available.

WebSocket HubAuto-Banter AIOBS WebSocketRTMP
✨

Reactions — AI Reaction Studio

Paste a YouTube URL, choose an angle ("wholesome roast, playful analysis, hype commentary"), pick VTuber position (Bottom Left / Center / Right) and beats count — AI generates a timestamped script and records the video.

Duo ModeAuto-BanterVisual AnalysisWhisper
🖼️

Art — AI Image Generator

Generate character art via Stable Diffusion with full control: sampler, width/height, CFG scale, seed, batch sizes (1, 4, 10, 25, 50, 💯), HR upscaling, face restore, meme mode with top/bottom text, and auto emotion detection.

Stable DiffusionMeme ModeHR UpscalingBatch 1–100
🎨

Thumbnails — Smart Generator

Auto-find the best frame with AI, apply styles (default / vibrant / dramatic / soft / neon), add text overlays, compare styles side-by-side, and batch generate for your entire queue at once.

AI Frame Detection5 StylesBatch GenerateStyle Compare
📊

Analytics — Insights Dashboard

Track YouTube Videos, Instagram Posts, Total Views, Total Likes, Total Comments, and Engagement Rate. See top 3 performing content by platform, monthly activity, best posting times, and top emojis.

YouTube APIInstagramPDF ExportCSV Export
🔍

SEO — Title & Keyword Optimizer

Real-time SEO score (A/B/C/D grade), keywords found, hooks found, emojis found. AI generates viral titles with style presets, analyzes descriptions (word count, CTAs, timestamps, hashtags), and suggests keywords.

SEO ScoreAI TitlesKeywordsDescription Analyzer
📈

Trends — Real-Time Monitoring

Filter by region (US, Japan, UK, Korea…) and niche (VTuber, Gaming, Anime, React, ASMR, Music). See rising queries, virality scores (High/Medium/Low), and one-click create content from any trending keyword.

9 Regions6 NichesVirality ScoresContent Ideas
💬

Interactions — Inbox

Unified inbox for unanswered comments & mentions across YouTube, Instagram, and Twitter. AI drafts replies, tracks follower deltas per platform, and lets you mark interactions answered with one click.

AI Reply DraftFollower DeltasMark AnsweredMulti-platform
💖

Superchats — Revenue Dashboard

Log donations from YouTube, Twitch, and Fanbox. USD-normalized revenue comparison, monthly growth charts, currency mix, top supporter rankings, 3-month forecast with trend indicator, and CSV/JSON exports.

USD NormalizeRevenue ForecastRankingsCSV Export
🛡️

Admin — User & CORS Management

Create users, assign roles (Creator → Editor → Manager → Moderator → Admin), reset passwords, and configure CORS allowed origins. Rate-limited logins with Discord alerts after 3 failed attempts.

5 RolesCORS ConfigHMAC SessionsDiscord Alerts

Auto-Bot — Overnight Content Goblin

At 2 AM JST, Daily mode cooks tomorrow’s three Shorts plus three social posts for every connected platform. Buffer mode keeps a ready-to-publish stash so the calendar never looks abandoned.

Daily + BufferEditorial QueueJST SchedulingBest-Time Slots
